2016 Gaming Room

The AnomalyCon Gaming Room will be open:
Friday: 2pm-11pm
Saturday: 10am-6pm
Sunday 10am-4pm

This year, we have a great collection of board and card games available for you to check out and play with your friends. Whether you want to play an old favorite or something you’ve never tried before, we’ll have volunteers there to help you get started!

And we’ve got our best role-playing schedule ever! There will be at least two RPGs going on at a time, so whether you’ve never played before or you just want to try something new, we’ve got your game! Beginners are welcome at all of our games.

We’ll be featuring several Steampunk and Victoriana-style games, as well as classic D&D adventures and alt-history too! Join noted game designers Sean Patrick Fannon and Ross Watson as they demonstrate the feverishly-anticipated Savage Rifts!

The complete schedule is listed below:

While it could be nothing, the Watchmen never take a chance. You must go to the highlands to get to the bottom of this phenomenon. Be successful and you might well be enlisted to join the ranks of the Watchmen. It’s fast, furious, steampunk fun in the world of The Widening Gyre. One of your number has been summoned to Italy as an heir to the estate of a famed concert pianist. However, all are shocked to find everything was left to his very young (and notoriously unfaithful) widow. His remaining family beg you to investigate. When the late musician’s grave is disturbed and then people start turning up dead around the mansion, it’s up to you to uncover what’s really going on! Rippers Resurrected is a Victoriana Horror table-top role-playing game for the Savage Worlds rules system. No experience necessary and characters will be provided. Savage Worlds Sara Martinez
