Welcome to AnomalyCon 2013!

Greetings Everyone! The day of the convention has finally arrived!
If you didn’t preregister, remember that you can still pick up single day or weekend passes at the con! A full weekend is only $35 for adults and $18 for children aged 6-13!

Greetings! Many public service announcements!

First of all, dear fans, let it be known that the schedule for our programming is very nearly complete. As soon as we’ve run it by all our wonderful guests this year, we’ll get it posted so you can begin planning your stay.

There are still rooms available at the Doubletree for only $80/night–make sure you book up at the link under “Location” at the top of our website.

Unfortunately, due to health concerns (and a stolen band gear problem), BB Blackdog and Professor Elemental are both currently unable to travel to the States. They both send their regrets. We are completing negotiations with a suitable replacement, and the tea dueling will still go on! We’ll officially announce our amazing guest shortly. We hope you will still support BB Blackdog in their campaign to replace their audio equipment.

In addition, we are running 9.5 tracks of programming this year. You asked for more writing workshops, and we’re giving them to you. Bring your laptops, your notepads, your writing utensils, even your cameras–you’re in for a weekend of creative instruction and wonderment!
