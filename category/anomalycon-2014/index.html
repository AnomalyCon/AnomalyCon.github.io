Thank You!

Thanks to everyone who showed up at AnomalyCon. We had a great time running the convention for you! If you were not able to pre-reg at the con, don’t fear! We’re opening up pre-reg for 2015 next week. It’s still a great low price, so don’t forget to check back before the price goes up.

Preregistration pickup opens at 1pm on Friday and regular registration opens at 2pm.
The vendor’s room is open from 3pm-8pm for preregistered attendees, and 4pm-8pm for other attendees.
On Friday programming starts at 2pm and the big concert starts at 6pm.

On Saturday registration opens at 8am and will remain open until 8pm.
The vendor’s room is open 9am-8pm.
Programming starts at 8am and runs until midnight, with the costume contest at 6pm and the big concert starting at 7pm.

On Sunday registration is open from 9am-5pm but switches to pre-reg for next year at 2pm.
The vendor’s room is open from 10am-4pm.
Programming starts at 9am, ends when closing ceremonies starts at 4pm, and includes the Lego Contest, the Balcony judging, and the Grand Tea (which starts at 12 noon).

Of course these are just a few highlights–make sure you check out the complete schedule under “Main Programming.”

During the course of the convention, keep an eye on our tumblr at http://anomalycon.tumblr.com/ to see our Live Blogger’s pictures!

ANOMALYCON 2015 BRINGS STEAMPUNK SCI-FI/FANTASY TO DENVER TECH CENTER HYATT

This year’s AnomalyCon features the theme “More Science Than Fiction”, with specialists on neuroscience, paranormal science, the science of time and MORE! The Geek Rock band The DoubleClicks will be joined by Psyche Corporation, Pandora Celtica, and Keldari Station to explore every nuance of Steampunk all through the night.

Don’t miss Cory Doctorow, S.J. Chambers, Molly Tanzer, Jason Heller, Mary Anne Mohanraj, Sherri L. Smith, Jody Lynn Nye and dozens more incredible authors as they bring science to life on the page and introduce you to alternate history–and future–as you’ve never experienced it before. Solve a scientifically baffling murder mystery and win fabulous prizes, bring your best costumes for the Grand Costume Contest, participate in our LEGO building or mustache grooming contests, and so much more. AnomalyCon is where Science, Technology, Education and Art Meet.

New Guest Announcements – And Tracy Hickman is coming to town with his Killer Breakfast!

We have a whole bundle of new guests to announce–including the fact that Pandora Celtica is once again joining us! We’re excited to have them!

This year’s Spotlight Guests now include Kevin J. Anderson, Tracy Hickman, and Trai Cartwright!

This year’s Spotlight Guests now include Kevin J. Anderson, Tracy Hickman, and Trai Cartwright!

Tracy Hickman is well known to the gaming and fantasy community, especially for his Dragonlance novel series. He also hosts a really cool event called Killer Breakfast, and this year we’re bringing it to AnomalyCon! Saturday morning at 9AM for an extra $5 attendees can join Tracy for donuts, coffee, and the chance to pit their level one characters against ultimate death! Here’s a cool explanation of what Killer Breakfast looks like.

Kevin J Anderson is probably one of the most published writers of our time. He’s well on his way to overtaking Isaac Asimov, and he’s certainly one of the best sources we can think of for both self publishing and big name publishing. He’s written Dune, Star Wars, Steampunk, and so much more.

Trai Cartwright is singlehandedly the coolest lady you haven’t met yet. She’s been batting in the big leagues of Hollywood for years, written, edited and pitched every genre under the sun, and she teaches amazing masterwork classes to teens and grownups alike. She knows how to hit it big, and she’s here to share the knowledge.

Peter J Wacks is the local expert on time travel, or so his doppelganger would have you believe. We’ve heard from his writing side, but this year he’s here to talk about self publishing, editing, and more.

Our programming this year is growing by leaps and bounds, and we’re so excited to share all of our writing, editing, publishing and business tracks with our audience. Don’t miss all the fun! Pre-registration is still only $25 for the whole weekend, and tickets for Killer Breakfast are available at registration!
